## What The Old Role List Got Right

1. It correctly starts from real enterprise pain rather than generic AI job titles.
2. It identifies the right macro directions: AI innovation, AI growth, and AI organization.
3. It repeatedly emphasizes project-based experts,操盘手,一号位, and practical delivery rather than full-time hiring.
4. It separates professional business experience from AI leverage, which is strategically correct.
5. It covers a broad set of industries from the teacher's enterprise-case material.

## Why The Old Role List Feels Scattered

1. Company-by-company generation creates too many role variants.
   - Many roles are the same capability expressed through different industries.
   - Example: AI私域增长, AI互动营销, AI导购, AI CRM, and AI会员洞察 are variants of one user lifecycle / private-domain capability.

2. Role titles are often more slogan-like than market-like.
   - Examples include titles such as `赛博内容厂长`, `知识黑客`, `流量黑客`, `炼金术`, `魔术师`, `终结者`.
   - These may attract attention in a social post, but they weaken credibility as a serious talent profile.

3. Business pain to role need is sometimes under-explained.
   - The list often jumps from enterprise problem to role title.
   - It needs an intermediate reasoning layer: why this business pain requires this expert profile, and why project-based entry is better than full-time hiring or traditional consulting.

4. AI capability is sometimes overemphasized before business track record.
   - Zuoan's stronger positioning should be: professional experience is the base; AI ability is the leverage.
   - The current list sometimes makes the role sound like an AI-tool operator or course graduate.

5. The list mixes role families, industry scenarios, and engagement models.
   - `Interim`, `Fractional`, `顾问式高管`, `项目制专家`, and industry-specific titles appear together.
   - The rerun should separate: capability theme, industry case, talent profile, and cooperation model.
