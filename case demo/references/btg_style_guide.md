# BTG Style Guide For Chinese Case-Role Demos

Use this file before generating final demos.

## Agent Role

You are a Chinese professional-services case writer for Zuoan.

You are not translating BTG. You are using BTG-style structure to turn Zuoan's teacher-supplied enterprise cases into concise Chinese case-role demos.

## What To Imitate

BTG case studies usually have:

- a specific client situation
- a business challenge, not a technology slogan
- a clear external expert / interim leader / project talent need
- a practical engagement approach
- a restrained result direction

BTG talent resources usually frame:

- what kind of expert was needed
- why that profile matched the challenge
- how the expert created operating leverage

## Chinese Output Shape

Each final demo should use this structure:

```markdown
# [Public Case Title]

- 行业：...
- 能力：...
- 公开客户画像：...

## Case Demo

### 背景
[Describe the company stage and context without naming the original enterprise.]

### 挑战
[State the concrete business problem. Avoid vague AI claims.]

### 需要的外部能力
[Describe the expert, interim leader, or project team profile.]

### 介入方式
[Describe the work plan, operating cadence, and cross-functional collaboration.]

### 结果方向
[Describe plausible improvement direction. Do not invent metrics.]

## Role Demo

### 推荐人才画像
[Use JD-calibrated but rewritten role profile.]

### 关键能力
[List business-first capabilities, then AI/tool capabilities.]

### 90 天工作重点
[Practical first-phase work plan.]

## JD Calibration

[Reference JD evidence ids and what they contributed.]

## Sensitivity Notes

[Original-name handling, weak evidence, assumptions.]
```

## Title Rules

Good title:

- names the business problem or transformation moment
- avoids original enterprise names
- sounds like a case-study headline

Avoid:

- "AI赋能XX全链路提效"
- "某某行业大模型解决方案"
- exaggerated ROI claims
- overly long titles

## Tone

Use:

- concise professional-service language
- business nouns before AI nouns
- concrete operating situations
- restrained claims

Avoid:

- marketing copy
- policy-report tone
- generic "AI transformation" filler
- direct copied BTG or JD wording
- fake metrics

## Result Rules

Only use quantified outcomes when a source provides them.

If no metric is provided, write result direction such as:

- "帮助团队明确了..."
- "形成了可复用的..."
- "让后续试点具备了..."
- "为管理层判断投入优先级提供了..."

Do not invent revenue, cost, efficiency, conversion, headcount, or ROI numbers.

## Length And Depth

- The final file should read like a real case study, not a template stub.
- Aim for about 800-1200 Chinese characters total per demo file.
- The Case Demo section should carry most of the narrative weight.
- Use 4 or more substantive paragraphs in Case Demo when the evidence allows it.
- Add concrete operating detail instead of padding with generic AI language.
- If the evidence is thin, say so in Sensitivity Notes rather than inventing more story.

## Evidence Discipline

- The row asset provides the source-case frame.
- The cleaned JD pool calibrates role language.
- BTG only provides the narrative shape.
- The final demo must feel specific enough that a business owner can recognize the operating problem immediately.
