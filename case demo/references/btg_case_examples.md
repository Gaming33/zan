## Example 1: AI Market Opportunity / Growth Strategy

- Source: https://resources.businesstalentgroup.com/btg-case-studies/ai-thought-leader-to-assess-market-opportunities-and-support-growth-strategy-efforts-in-tech
- Client pattern: technology company evaluating AI-related market opportunities.
- Problem pattern: leadership needed sharper judgment on where AI could create commercial opportunity.
- Expert pattern: AI thought leader / market expert with strategic and technical fluency.
- Work pattern: assess opportunities, support growth strategy, help decision-makers prioritize.
- Reusable structure: market uncertainty -> expert judgment -> opportunity map -> management decision support.
- Zuoan mapping: AI cloud private deployment, gov-enterprise AI solution delivery, smart agriculture POC.
