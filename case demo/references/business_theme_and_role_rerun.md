# Business Theme And Role Rerun

Use this reference to extract business themes and diagnose the teacher's old role list.

## Business Theme Extraction

Primary source:

```text
C:\Users\Leo\Downloads\50家企业案例+++AI提效.docx
```

Extract from the industry distribution table:

- industry cluster
- representative companies
- business model traits
- core pain points
- urgent AI application scenarios
- initial talent gaps
- urgency / value signal

Do not invent themes from JD data. JD data can only validate or refine talent capability themes after business themes are extracted.

## Business Theme Shape

Each business theme should be written as:

```markdown
### [Theme Name]
- Source industry cluster:
- Representative teacher cases:
- Business pain:
- AI application scenario:
- Why this is a Zuoan case:
- Initial talent gap from teacher material:
- Related demo targets:
```

Prefer 6-8 business themes. Avoid making one theme per company.

## Old Role-List Diagnosis

Primary source:

```text
C:\Users\Leo\Downloads\岗位清单.docx
```

Diagnose the old role list before rerunning it.

Look for:

- roles split too finely by company instead of capability
- role titles that sound like AI slogans rather than market roles
- repeated capability behind different industry labels
- missing reasoning between enterprise pain and role need
- AI capability overemphasized while business track record is underdefined
- role descriptions that do not sound like real high-quality market recruiting

## Rerun Rule

Rerun roles as talent profiles anchored in:

```text
teacher business pain + teacher old role intent + JD evidence + project-based Zuoan delivery model
```

Do not rerun roles from JD evidence alone.

## Capability Theme Design

After business themes and JD evidence are available, derive capability themes at the intersection:

```text
business pain x AI application scenario x market talent capability
```

Examples:

- fresh food loss control x demand forecasting x supply-chain AI optimization expert
- mother-baby member repurchase x private-domain agent x AI growth operator
- vocational education bottleneck x intelligent grading x AI education product lead

Use capability themes as the skeleton. Use industry cases as evidence. Do not let the final structure become 50 scattered role titles.

## Output Contracts

When asked for business theme extraction, write:

```text
outputs/business_theme_report.md
```

When asked for role-list diagnosis, write:

```text
outputs/role_list_diagnosis.md
```

`role_list_diagnosis.md` must include:

- what the old role list got right
- why it feels scattered
- which roles can be merged into capability themes
- which roles are strong demo candidates
- suggested rerun structure
